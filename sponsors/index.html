Details on sponsorship levels are listed below. Please consider donating or becoming an active sponsor of our group. If you are an individual or organization willing to support the community through us, please send an email to sponsorship@gsjug.org.

Sponsorship Levels

Bronze $500/year Benefits of Bronze Sponsorship includes:
  • Your logo with link displayed on our sponsors page with appropriate level
  • Your logo displayed on our introduction slide before each meetup session
  • Social media reshare of information that is relevant to the Java community
  • Share material swag at in-person meetups, e-swag at online versions
Silver $1000/year Benefits of Silver Sponsorship includes:
  • All the benefits of Bronze Sponsorship, plus...
  • Post job opportunities via email distribution group of about 300
  • Booth at in-person meetups to network and discuss, social media handles for online
Gold $1500/year Benefits of Gold Sponsorship includes:
  • All the benefits of Silver Sponsorship, plus...
  • Social media re-share of job opportunities and advertisements
  • Address audience for two minutes, twice a year, before the meetup session
  • Provision to host a workshop/session during the year
Platinum $1500/year Benefits of Platinum Sponsorship includes:
  • All the benefits of Gold Sponsorship, plus...
  • Address audience for five minutes, four times a year, before the meetup session
  • Provision to host two workshops/sessions during the year
  • Consideration for potential speaker recommendations
  • Logo/name displayed on front page
Please note that Platinum Level sponsorship is limited to only one organization per year
Partner   Benefits of Partner Sponsorship includes:
  • All the benefits of Gold Sponsorship, plus...
  • Advisory committee membership for GSJUG
Per Meeting Licenses, Swag, Food Benefits of Per Meeting Sponsorship includes:
  • Your logo with link displayed on our sponsors page with appropriate level
  • Your logo displayed on our introduction slide before each meetup session
  • Share swag at the sponsored meetup
