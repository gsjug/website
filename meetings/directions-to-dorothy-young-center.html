From Downtown Madison

  • Madison Avenue (NJ State Highway 124) towards Morristown.
  • Make a LEFT onto Lancaster Road.
    • NOTE: the opposite side is called Elm Street.
  • Tilghman Parking Lot will be to your RIGHT with two (2) entrances:
    • the first entrance is before the guard house.
    • the second entrance is after the guard house.
    • NOTE: the first entrance is occasionally blocked off.
  • After parking, cross Lancaster Road and walk on the sidewalk in between the faculty parking lot and the back of the Dorothy Young Center for the Arts.
    • you will approach a courtyard.
  • Look to your LEFT and you will see an entrance with a dome.
    • this is the entrance to the Dorothy Young Center for the Arts.
  • After entering the building, go straight through to the end of the hallway.
  • Make a LEFT and walk about 100 feet.
    • Room 106 will be on your RIGHT.
    • NOTE: Room 106 is after Room 108.

From Downtown Morristown and I-287

  • Madison Avenue (NJ State Highway 124) towards Madison.
  • Make a RIGHT onto Lancaster Road.
    • NOTE: the opposite side is called Elm Street.
  • Follow directions from above.
