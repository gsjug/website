June 24, 2025
Testing GenAI Applications in Java with Testcontainers and Docker

We are happy to announce that Anna Chernyshova will be our special guest for this month's meeting.

The AI landscape is evolving fast. For Java developers, libraries like LangChain4j and Spring AI simplify integration, but testing remains a major challenge. I'll walk you through how Testcontainers and Docker can streamline your testing and development workflows for GenAI applications.

You'll learn how to create isolated, reproducible environments to test AI behaviors and RAG workflows with confidence, and how to leverage the Docker Model Runner to enhance your local experience of GenAI-powered app development.
