October 2014 Meeting Information

October 14, 2014
A Review of the JavaOne Conference

This month, we will have a formal review of the JavaOne conference. This year's conference was held from September 29 through October 2 and our own Barry Burd was in attendance. He will provide a full report to let us know what is new and exciting in the Java world. We can also review some of the sessions that are available from the JavaOne web site.
