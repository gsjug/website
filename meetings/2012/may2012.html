Technical Overview of the Twitter API: An Update

When we last discussed the Twitter API in November 2009, it was fairly easy to setup authentication. However, that has since changed. Getting access to your Twitter account now involves obtaining special keys similar to that with Facebook. In this month''s meeting, we will discuss these changes and review the steps necessary to access your Twitter account and information about your account.
