  2. Directions to Hall of Sciences

From downtown Madison:

  • Madison Avenue (SH 124) towards Morristown.
  • Make a LEFT onto Campus Drive.
    • NOTE: there is no street sign, but the landmark is the United Methodist Church in Madison immediately before Campus Drive.
  • After passing the first two small buildings on your RIGHT, make a RIGHT onto the road leading to the Hall of Sciences parking lot.
    • NOTE: the parking lot is directly behind the second building that you passed.
  • After entering the building, make your way to the second floor and head towards the back of the building.
  • Make a RIGHT at the last hallway.
    • Room 244 will be on your LEFT.

From downtown Morristown and I-287:

  • Madison Avenue (SH 124) towards Madison.
  • Make a RIGHT onto Campus Drive.
    • NOTE: there is no street sign, but the landmark is the United Methodist Church in Madison immediately after Campus Drive.
  • Follow directions from above.
