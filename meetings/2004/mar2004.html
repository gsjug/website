March 2004 Meeting Information

March 9, 2004
Technical Overview of J#.NET

Manny Goyenechea will be making a presentation on J#.NET which will focus on the migration of Java applications to J# and the migration of J# applications to Java. Manny will demonstrate how to port simple Java applications to J# and simple J# applications to Java. There will be a detailed review at the similarities and differences of the two languages and runtime environments, and point out some of the pitfalls. Also, there will be an overview of .NET interoperability and at developing a J# application with VisualStudio .NET.

Meetings are held every second Tuesday of the month at the Scotch Plains Rescue Squad second floor meeting hall starting promptly at 7:30pm. Meetings are open to the public, and visitors are encouraged to join ACGNJ.
