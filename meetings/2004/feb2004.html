Java Users Group

February 2004 Meeting Information

February 10, 2004
Top 15 Ant Best Practices / Introduction to Tapestry

Paul Furbacher will making his main presentation on the Top 15 Ant Best Practices based on the December 17, 2003 O'Reilly OnJava article written by Eric M. Burke. He will then provide an introduction to Tapestry, a powerful, open-source, all-Java framework for creating leading edge web applications in Java. It is an alternative to scripting environments such as JavaServer Pages (JSPs) as extremely dynamic applications can be developed with a minimal amount of coding.
