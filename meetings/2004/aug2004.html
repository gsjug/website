August 2004 Meeting Information

August 10, 2004
Yearly Review & Planning Meeting

This month's meeting will be an annual review of some of the more interesting topics that have been covered over the past year. We will also discuss potential topics for the 2004–2005 "season."

Meetings are held every second Tuesday of the month at the Scotch Plains Rescue Squad second floor meeting hall starting promptly at 7:30pm. Meetings are open to the public, and visitors are encouraged to join ACGNJ.
