April 2021 [a] Meeting Information

April 13, 2021
Demystifying Open-Source

We are happy to announce that Neha Sardana will be our special guest for this month's meeting.

Contributing to open source software can be intimidating. It is important to know where to start. This session will help you demystify the myths of contributing to open source. Neha will teach you the fundamentals of how to choose a project to contribute and leverage the usage of Github. At the end of the session, you will have the skills and knowledge of working with open source projects needed to make high-quality contributions you will be proud of.
