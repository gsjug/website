Garden State JUG 25th Anniversary Celebration

On February 9, 2001, Mike Redlich kicked off the very first Java User Group meeting in Scotch Plains, New Jersey. What started as a few Java enthusiasts hacking code at each meeting has evolved into providing quality monthly presentations delivered by Java luminaries.

Come celebrate with us as we commemorate our 25th anniversary with special in-person guests, so for, Sharat Chander and Venkat Subramaniam. We hope to have additional special guests to remotely join us. Please stay tuned for more details!

Pizza and soft drinks will be served. We are also working on cake and cupcakes featuring our logo!
