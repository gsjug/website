Unlocking Contextual Intelligence in Geospatial Monitoring: A Converged Spatial + AI Vector Approach with Oracle Database 26ai

We are happy to announce that Doug Drechsel will be our special guest for this month's meeting.

Traditional geospatial monitoring systems excel at enforcing boundaries through proven technologies like Oracle Spatial, but they represent a foundation with tremendous unlocked potential. What if these systems could distinguish between routine events - like a vehicle rerouted through a restricted zone - and genuine risk behaviors - like loitering near that same zone? This is the promise of contextual intelligence.

This session introduces Prescience, a proof-of-concept system that demonstrates how Oracle Database 26ai's converged architecture transforms geospatial monitoring from reactive boundary enforcement into a proactive digital partner for security and parole officers. By fusing native Spatial capabilities with AI Vector Search in a single, streamlined workflow, we show how to enhance existing geofencing with behavioral pattern recognition.
