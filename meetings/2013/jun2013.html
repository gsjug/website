June 11, 2013
Technical Overview of AppDynamics

I am happy to announce that Chris Carr from AppDynamics will be making a presentation about their application performance monitoring and management tool. AppDynamics will be an official sponsor of this month's meeting. Hear what the President of AppDynamics, Joe Sexton, has to say about the Company.

Pizza, soft drinks, and cookies will be served! Also, Chris will bring mugs, shirts, and maybe even some jackets for swag to give away! Please RSVP to mike@redlich.net so that an accurate count for pizza can be provided.

Meetings are held every second Tuesday of the month at the Scotch Plains Rescue Squad second floor meeting hall starting promptly at 7:30pm. Meetings are open to the public, and visitors are encouraged to join ACGNJ.
