January 9, 2018
Introduction to Java 9

We are happy to announce that Jason Young will be our special guest for this month's meeting.

Java 9 is out and it contains 82 specific enhancements to the VM, language and API! Jason will talk about some great new features that have been developed over the last four years including Jigsaw, HTTP 2.0 support, REPL, and more!

Meetings are held every second Tuesday of the month at the Dorothy Young Center for the Arts, Room 106, at Drew University starting promptly at 6:30pm. Meetings are open to the public, and visitors are encouraged to join ACGNJ.
