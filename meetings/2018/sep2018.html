September 11, 2018
Kotlin 101

Barry Burd will present Kotlin 101.

The Kotlin language, developed by JetBrains, has been adopted as a first-class platform for Android application development. In this talk, Barry will introduce Kotlin programming and compare features of Kotlin with their counterparts in Java. He will also demonstrate the creation of simple apps for Android phones.

Meetings are held every second Tuesday of the month at the Dorothy Young Center for the Arts, Room 106, at Drew University starting promptly at 6:30pm. Meetings are open to the public, and visitors are encouraged to join ACGNJ.
