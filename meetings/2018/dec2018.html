December 17, 2018
Java Open Source Panel and Technical Overview of Flutter

We are happy to announce that Chandra Guntur and Donald Raab will join us to participate in a Java panel on contributing to open-source projects.

Also, we are happy to announce that Scott Stoll and Nash Ramdial will remotely join us (via Zoom) to demonstrate Flutter, an open-source mobile application development SDK created by Google.

Meetings are held every second Tuesday of the month at the Dorothy Young Center for the Arts, Room 106, at Drew University starting promptly at 6:30pm. Meetings are open to the public, and visitors are encouraged to join ACGNJ.
