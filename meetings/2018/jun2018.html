June 2018 Meeting Information

June 12, 2018
Demystifying Microservices for Java EE Developers

Microservices has been a popular topic these past few years and there has been much debate over their implementation and migrating a monolith applications. Mike will introduce microservices, discuss their advantages and disadvantages, why Java EE/Jakarta EE can be used for microservices, and provide a demonstration of a microservices application deployed with Payara Micro.
