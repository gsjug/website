August 2006 Meeting Information

August 8, 2006
Special Project: Database Application "Bake-Off"

We will be holding a database "bake-off" where small teams will develop a database application using their favorite object-relational bridge (Hibernate, Cayenne, etc.). To make the most efficient use of time, a small sports MySQL 5.0 database and corresponding POJOs to match the database tables have already been created. The mission will be to use a tool such as Hibernate and Cayenne to complete the mapping and obtain some result sets. You can download the zip file containing everything you need to get things set up before the meeting (please read the readme.txt file first). Also, there will be presentations by Tatjana from Carnagie Associates and Luz Sandoval from Robert Half Technology.

Meetings are held every second Tuesday of the month at the Scotch Plains Rescue Squad second floor meeting hall starting promptly at 7:30pm. Meetings are open to the public, and visitors are encouraged to join ACGNJ.
