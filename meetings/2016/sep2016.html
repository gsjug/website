September 2016 Meeting Information

September 13, 2016
Plex Cloud ERP, SQL, Report Writing

Bill will present and demonstrate a browser-based business system for manufacturing, Plex. Plex has a new UX (User Experience) front-end and a Microsoft database back-end. The coding focus of this talk is SPROCS (SQL Stored PROCedureS). Bill will demo and discuss:
  • Report Writers
  • VisionPlex (JavaScript)
  • Information Builders - InfoAssist
  • WebServices
  • Plex Community
ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)
SQL (Structured Query Language)

Bill Brutzman is an aspiring programmer at a manufacturing company in Bergen County.
