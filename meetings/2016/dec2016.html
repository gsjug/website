December 13, 2016
GoLang 101 ‐ Thinking in Go

"Wild Bill" Brutzman will present his thoughts on the Go programming language. During his presentation, he will answer these burning questions:
  • Who...are Tony Hoare and Rob Pike?
  • What...are the profound new ideas in Go?
  • When...did Go come to life??
  • Where...Go is coming from and where Go shines?
  • Why...Go is better, faster, stronger, leaner, cleaner, easier ‐ a game-changer.

Meetings are held every second Tuesday of the month at the Scotch Plains Rescue Squad second floor meeting hall starting promptly at 7:30pm. Meetings are open to the public, and visitors are encouraged to join ACGNJ.
