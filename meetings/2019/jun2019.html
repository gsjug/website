June 17, 2019
Technical Overview of Okta Java SDK

Okta adds authentication, authorization, and user management to your web or mobile app within minutes. They provides companies with Workforce Identity and Customer Identity. As stated on the website, the Okta Identity Cloud is “an independent and neutral platform that securely connects the right people to the right technologies at the right time.”

Developers are encouraged to created their own forever-free account to build their own Okta-driven applications.

There will be a brief overview of Okta including how to establish an Okta account and a demo of simple web and command-line applications.
