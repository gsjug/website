February 2022 Meeting Information

February 10, 2022
MicroProfile 4.1, 5.0 and Beyond

We are happy to announce that Emily Jiang will be our special guest for this month's meeting.

MicroProfile 4.1 was released in July 2021 and 5.0 was released in December 2021. This session will bring you up to date with the latest release of MicroProfile with a live demo and showcase the latest release of MicroProfile. Finally, Emily will discuss the future direction of MicroProfile.
