June 14, 2022
Coding That Sparks Joy With Quarkus

We are happy to announce that Edson Yanaga will be our special guest for this month's meeting.

Does your heart beat faster when you're developing your Java application? Does it spark joy when you're coding? If not, then it's time to leave some old ways behind.

Join us in this live coding session and get familiar with Quarkus. You'll learn to love how your old and new favorite APIs will start in a matter of milliseconds and consume tiny amounts of memory. Hot reload capabilities for development will give you instant sparks of joy!
