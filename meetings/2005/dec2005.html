December 13, 2005
Technical Overview of xProcess

This month, Chris Page from Ivis Technologies will visit us to demonstrate their latest product, xProcess. Development managers complain that they lack visibility and control over their projects. They spend a great deal of time putting together an elaborate initial plan, only to see the situation changing as requirements, estimates, priorities or resources are modified. The problem: They are unable to update their initial plan and/or process with real-time feedback, to reflect how well or poorly reality matches the initial assumptions. Inefficient ways of working may be perpetuated, while efficient approaches may be lost and must be re-discovered over and over again.

Chris Page, Director of Services for Ivis Technologies, will be presenting xProcess and demonstrating how xProcess can help you solve these problems. xProcess is the premier process improvement and project management environment that maximizes the success of software development and other projects. By combining process specification, plan generation and live project execution into one product, xProcess not only lets teams define their development processes but captures, modifies, and improves those processes, while continually monitoring the projects as they evolve.

Everyone who attends will receive an xProcess CD.
