Java Users Group

March 2003 Meeting Information

March 11, 2003
Developing Dynamic Web Applications with JavaBeans and JavaServer Pages

This month's meeting will be a demonstration on how to dynamically build a web application using JavaBeans and JavaServer Pages (JSPs). We will review a few small applications along with an overview of JavaBeans and the Tomcat web server.

Meetings are held every second Tuesday of the month at the Scotch Plains Rescue Squad second floor meeting hall starting promptly at 7:30pm. Meetings are open to the public, and visitors are encouraged to join ACGNJ.
