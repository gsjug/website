April 8, 2003
Technical Overview of Salmon LLC's SOFIA Framework

This month's meeting will be presented by Ken Hoffman, Vice President, Sales, and a lead architect from Salmon LLC to provide an overview and demonstration of SOFIA (Salmon Open Framework for Internet Applications). SOFIA was developed in-house, is conceptually similar to Jakarta Struts, and provides:
  • integration of tools such as IntelliJ Idea, Eclipse, and Dreamweaver.
  • simplifies code by providing over 200,000 lines of ready-to-go code based on the Model-View-Controller (MVC) design pattern as well as the separation of HTML and JSP.
  • productivity improvement.

More information about SOFIA and how to download the product is available at the Salmon LLC web site.
