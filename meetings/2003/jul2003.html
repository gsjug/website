Java Users Group

July 2003 Meeting Information

July 8, 2003
Yearly Review & Planning Meeting

This month's meeting will be an annual review of some of the more interesting topics that have been covered over the past year. This will be the first annual review since the user group was formed in February 2001, and subsequent annual reviews will be scheduled for every July meeting. Some of the previous topics include Ant, Digester, EJBs, and three different software design pattern topics. Also, representatives from SilverStream (now Novell) and Salmon LLC have visited us in June 2002 and April 2003, respectively.
