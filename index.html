Coding That Sparks Joy With Quarkus

Abstract

Does your heart beat faster when you're developing your Java application? Does it spark joy when you're coding? If not, then it's time to leave some old ways behind.

Join us in this live coding session and get familiar with Quarkus. You'll learn to love how your old and new favorite APIs will start in a matter of milliseconds and consume tiny amounts of memory. Hot reload capabilities for development will give you instant sparks of joy!

Bio

Edson Yanaga, Red Hat's Director of Developer Experience, is a Java Champion and a Microsoft MVP. He is also a published author and a frequent speaker at international conferences, discussing Java, Kubernetes, Quarkus, Microservices, Cloud Computing, and DevOps.

Edson believes that everything we do, big or small, matters - and that we can all create a better world for people with better software. His life's purpose is to deliver good software and to help developers worldwide in delivering better software faster and reliably. Today his job, his hobby, and his life’s purpose are the same thing: and he’s happy that he found his Ikigai.
