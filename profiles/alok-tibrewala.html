Alok Tibrewala

Alok Tibrewala is an IEEE Senior Member and seasoned Software Engineer with over nine years of expertise in enterprise-scale software architecture, Java-based application development, and AI integration. He holds an MS in Computer Science from The George Washington University and is an active independent researcher with focus on the intersection of Java ecosystems and Large Language Models (LLMs), including frameworks like LangChain4j.

Alok regularly contributes to the tech community through speaking engagements at IEEE Conferences, Hackathons (e.g., IEEE CAI, NJIT), peer reviews, and mentoring, sharing innovative insights and practical solutions from his research in AI-enhanced application intelligence.
