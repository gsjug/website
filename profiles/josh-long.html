Josh Long

Josh Long is the Spring Developer Advocate at WMware. Josh is a Java Champion, author of five books (including O'Reilly's upcoming “Cloud Native Java: Designing Resilient Systems with Spring Boot, Spring Cloud, and Cloud Foundry”) and three best-selling video trainings (including “Building Microservices with Spring Boot Livelessons” with Phil Webb, Staff Engineer 2 at VMWare), and an open-source contributor (Spring Boot, Spring Integration, Spring Cloud, Activiti and Vaadin).
