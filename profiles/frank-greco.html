Frank Greco

Frank Greco is a senior technology consultant and enterprise architect working on cloud and AI/ML tools for developers. He is a Java Champion, Chairman of the NYJavaSIG (first JUG ever), and runs the International Machine Learning for the Enterprise conference in Europe. Frank is co-author of JSR 381, Visual Recognition (VisRec) Specification, and strong advocate for Java and Machine Learning. He is a member of the NullPointers. #STEAMnotSTEM
