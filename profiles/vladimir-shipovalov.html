Vladimir Shipovalov

My introduction to computers began in the 90s when my parents bought a ZX Spectrum. I became familiar with Basic, then Pascal. At university, I studied C/C++. After graduating, I worked at a telecommunications company. I developed various systems using C++. Later, I got a little acquainted with C#.

Then there was a break in my development career. I joined an oil company where, in addition to administering computer systems, I created the country's most powerful computing cluster.

I decided to return to development because I enjoyed it more. I chose Java as a more promising language. I worked on several interesting projects in domains such as e-commerce and fintech.

I have been developing with Java for over 8 years.

Besides Java, I have also developed using Kotlin and Golang.
